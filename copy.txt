## About FBA:

Online shopping has come a long way from being a novelty to becoming the backbone of modern retail.

In the UK specifically, over 90% of people online have ditched the high streets in favour of armchair ordering, making the UK the third largest eCommerce market in the world after China and the US.

Almost 50p out of every £1 spent on shopping in the UK happens online.

So what better time to start FBA than NOW?

For those of you who have never heard of FBA (Fulfilment By Amazon) — it's one of the best biz models that allows you to work from your own surroundings, giving you access to Amazon's massive storage and logistics network.

Which means you outsource the annoying parts of running a business — handling orders, packing, posting — while all you have to do is SHOW UP with a product that flies off the shelves like hot pakoras!
